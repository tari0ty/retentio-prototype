📝 How to Play

  1. Flip a Card: Click on any face-down card to reveal the hidden image beneath it.
  2. Find its Match: Click a second card to flip it over.
  3. The Result: It's a Match! The cards stay face-up.
  4. Not a Match? Both cards automatically flip back face-down after a brief moment. Memorize their positions!
  5. Win the Game: Keep turning over pairs until the entire board is revealed.
