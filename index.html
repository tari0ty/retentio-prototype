📝 How to Play

  1. Flip a Card: Click any face-down card to reveal the hidden image.
  2. Find its Match: Click a second card to flip it over.
  3. It's a Match! Matched cards stay face-up.
  4. Not a Match? Both cards flip back face-down. Memorize their positions!
  5. Win the Game: Match all pairs to complete the level.
